About Us

Snowjilla Adventure was founded with the vision of bringing the spirit of adventure to travelers who seek to explore the untouched beauty of the Himalayas. It started as an idea in the backyard of our home over a discussion with a cup of tea. Founded by Ankit Negi in 2025, Snowjilla Adventure is a dream turned reality. With a zeal for adventure and extensive experience in trekking and adventure sports, Ankit is a certified MOI from ABVIMAS, Manali. He has conquered numerous expeditions, including Kinner Kailash, Friendship Peak, and Pin-Bhabha Pass. Our team, led by certified trek experts, ensures a safe and immersive experience in the mountains.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide unparalleled travel and tourism experiences that connect people with nature, adventure, and local cultures.

Our Vision

To become a leading adventure tourism provider, offering curated experiences that inspire wanderlust and create memories for a lifetime.
